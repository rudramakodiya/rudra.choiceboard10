🌋 My Funny Volcano Adventure 😂

📅 Date: 10 July 2026

Dear Diary,

Today our school decided that taking children to a volcano was a GREAT idea. Who thought this was safe?!

The volcano suddenly made a loud "BOOM!". I almost dropped my water bottle and my sandwich flew into a bush.

Our guide laughed and said, "Don't worry, it's not erupting." Easy for him to say... HE wasn't holding my sandwich!

Then I found a shiny black igneous rock. The guide explained that it was made when hot lava cooled down. Finally... something in science that sounded cool!

I wanted to keep the rock, but my mom said, "No. Your school bag already weighs enough."

By the end of the trip I learned that volcanoes create igneous rocks... but they are TERRIBLE picnic spots.

😂 Lesson of the Day:
If a volcano says "BOOM!", don't stop for a selfie!
